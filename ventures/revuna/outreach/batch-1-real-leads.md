## Cold call script (for phone-channel leads)

Keep it under 20 seconds before handing to a human decision — front desk staff will screen calls, so ask for the owner/office manager by name if known, otherwise:

> "Hi, this is [Name] — I help small dental practices keep up with Google reviews without it eating into anyone's day. Is [Owner name / 'the practice owner'] around, or is there a better time to catch them for 60 seconds?"

If they ask what it's about: "I built a tool that drafts review replies automatically so nothing sits unanswered — $19/month, no contract. Happy to just send a quick email instead if that's easier — who should I address it to?"

Goal of the call is not to sell on the spot — it's to get a name + email or a "no thanks," either of which is a real signal for the narrowest-bet test.
