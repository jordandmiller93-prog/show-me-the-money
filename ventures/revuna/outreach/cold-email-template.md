# Cold email template — narrowest-bet outreach batch

## CAN-SPAM compliance (required before sending, US-based commercial email)

- Truthful "From" name and subject line — no deception about content.
- Include a real physical mailing address in the footer (a registered agent address or your own is fine — required by law).
- Include a one-line opt-out ("reply STOP to opt out") and honor it within 10 business days.
- This is B2B outreach to publicly listed business contact emails, not a purchased list — keep it that way; don't scrape or buy bulk email lists.

## Subject lines (A/B, pick 2 per batch)

- "Your [Business Name] reviews — quick one"
- "Saw your review from [Reviewer first name / date]"
- "A reply for your unanswered {Google/Yelp} review"

## Template

```
Subject: Saw your review from {reviewer_first_name_or_date}

Hi {owner_first_name},

I noticed {business_name} has {unanswered_count} unanswered reviews on Google
Business Profile — including one from {reviewer_first_name} on {review_date}
that's been sitting for {days_unanswered} days.

I wrote a reply for that one so you can just copy-paste it if it's useful:

---
{ai_drafted_reply}
---

I'm building Revuna — it does this automatically for every new review, in
your voice, so nothing sits unanswered. $19/mo, no contract, cancel anytime
(most review tools in this space run $300-900/mo with annual contracts —
this isn't that).

Want your next 3 replies free, no card needed? Just reply "yes" and I'll set
it up.

{sender_first_name}
{physical_mailing_address}
Reply STOP to opt out of future emails.
```

## Personalization checklist (per prospect, before sending)

- [ ] Confirmed 4.0-4.7★ rating, 50+ total reviews, 3+ unanswered in last 90 days (targeting filter from STRATEGY.md)
- [ ] Real unanswered review identified — quote it accurately, don't paraphrase
- [ ] Draft reply written specifically for that review's content (see `sample-responses.md` for tone/quality bar) — never a generic template reply
- [ ] Owner's actual first name used (not "Business Owner" or "Team")
- [ ] Physical address and opt-out line present

## Tracking

Log every send in a simple sheet: business name, contact email, date sent, review quoted, reply status, converted (y/n). Needed to evaluate the narrowest-bet kill-switch on 2026-07-23 (see STRATEGY.md).
