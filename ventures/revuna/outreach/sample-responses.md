# Sample AI-drafted review responses

Demo assets for cold outreach — proof of drafting quality, not fabricated engagement metrics. Use these as the "here's what I'd write for you" attachment in emails, drafted against a fictional example business ("Bright Smile Dental") so nothing here impersonates a real customer or review.

Tone rules baked into every draft: specific (never generic "thank you for your feedback"), owner's voice (warm, not corporate), under 80 words, no defensive language even on negative reviews, always invites offline resolution for complaints instead of arguing in public.

---

### 5-star review
> "Dr. Patel and the whole team were amazing. Painless cleaning, they explained everything, and I was in and out in 30 minutes."

**Draft reply:**
"Thank you so much for this — it means a lot to hear the visit felt fast *and* thorough. I'll pass this along to Dr. Patel and the team. See you at your next cleaning!"

---

### 4-star review (mild complaint)
> "Good cleaning, friendly staff, but I waited almost 25 minutes past my appointment time."

**Draft reply:**
"Thanks for the honest feedback — glad the cleaning itself was a good experience. A 25-minute wait isn't the standard we want, and I'm looking into what happened with scheduling that day. Hope to see you again soon and make the wait a non-issue."

---

### 1-star review (angry)
> "Terrible experience. Was charged for a procedure I never agreed to. Will not be back."

**Draft reply:**
"I'm sorry to hear this — an unexpected charge is a serious concern and I want to get it sorted out directly. Could you call the office and ask for [Owner name] so I can look into your billing and make this right? That's not the experience we want anyone to have."

---

### 3-star review (neutral)
> "It was fine. Nothing special but nothing wrong either."

**Draft reply:**
"Appreciate you taking the time to leave a review. 'Fine' tells us there's room to make the visit stand out more next time — if there's anything specific we could've done better, I'd genuinely like to hear it."

---

### 2-star review (specific complaint)
> "Receptionist was rude on the phone when I tried to reschedule."

**Draft reply:**
"I'm sorry — that's not how we want anyone to be treated on a call with us, rescheduling or otherwise. I'll be addressing this with the front desk directly. If you're open to giving us another shot, I'd like the chance to make your next interaction a better one."
