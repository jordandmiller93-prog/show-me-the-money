# Text/SMS follow-up — send right after a call says "tell me more"

Works whether or not the website is live yet — this is the whole offer in plain text, no link required.

## Standard follow-up text

```
Hi [Name], this is Jordan from our call — here's the offer:

Revuna drafts replies to your Google reviews automatically, in your
voice. You approve with one tap before anything posts.

$19/mo (Google only) or $39/mo (+ Yelp/Facebook). No contract,
cancel anytime — most tools like this run $300-900/mo with a yearly
contract, this isn't that.

Want your next 3 replies free, no card needed? Just send me your
Google Business Profile link (or the business name + city) and I'll
send back drafted replies for your most recent reviews.
```

## If they ask for a website

```
Don't have a public site up yet, but happy to show you exactly what
a reply looks like — send me your Google Business Profile link (or
just the business name + city) and I'll draft a real reply to one of
your actual reviews so you can see it firsthand. No cost, no signup.
```

This sidesteps needing a live link entirely — the demo *is* a real drafted reply for their actual business, which is a stronger proof point than a generic landing page anyway.

## If they say yes and send a review

Bring it straight into this Claude Code session (or any future one) and ask for a drafted reply — see `CONCIERGE-SOP.md` for the full loop.
